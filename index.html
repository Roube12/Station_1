What is Length?

Length is defined as the distance between two points. Whether it's the size of an atom or the distance to stars, length helps us map our universe!

SI Base Unit: meter (m)

Challenge Lab

CHALLENGE QUESTION

Which tool is most suitable for measuring the diameter of a coin?

Hint: Think about how you grip circular objects securely to measure through the widest center point!

🎉

Correct Answer!

Vernier Calipers are perfect because their two jaws can clasp the outside of circular items like coins, making it easy to capture the exact diameter!
